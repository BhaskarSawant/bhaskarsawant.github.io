About

I am a Lead Engineer and Solution Architect with 15+ years of experience across enterprise software engineering, cloud-native systems, ERP-integrated platforms, AI-enabled systems, cybersecurity, and software modernization.

My work sits at the intersection of engineering execution and applied research: building platforms that are secure, observable, compliant, scalable, and maintainable in real production environments.
